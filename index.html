01 / What is PALLS?

Your backlog,
finally tamed.

Built at GoodTech for teams tired of sprint planning theatre and backlog debt that never shrinks.

PALLS is the Product Analyst Language Learning Syntax — an AI model purpose-built for the software development lifecycle.

It cuts through backlog noise, scoring every item by severity and business impact so your team always works on what matters most. Then it generates fully-formed JIRA stories with clear acceptance criteria your engineers can ship from — no follow-up meetings required.

Backlog Prioritisation

Ranks every item by severity and business impact — automatically, without the spreadsheet gymnastics.

JIRA Story Generation

Complete stories with acceptance criteria your engineers can ship from — no follow-up meetings required.

Analyst-Grade Intelligence

Trained on real product workflows. Knows the difference between a bug and a feature request.
